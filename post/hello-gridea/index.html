Hello, I'm Yinuo Liu (also call me Nicole). I am an undergraduate student in psychology based at the Zhejiang University, China (expected graduation: Autumn 2023).

I'm working as a research assistant both in CAI Lab and Kong Lab. In CAI Lab, my work focuses on the feature binding in working memory. In Kong Lab, my work focuses on language brain networks. And I have posted one of my works at the OHBM 2022 Annual Meeting.

You can email me for anything you are interested in: zjuonellllyn@gmail.com
