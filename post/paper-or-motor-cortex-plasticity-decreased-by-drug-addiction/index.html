Paper || Motor cortex plasticity decreased by drug addiction

作者: LIU Yinuo 日期: 2020-05-04 阅读时间:5.9 分钟 字数:1230 分类: paper

Heroin Addiction Impairs Human Cortical Plasticity

info

Drug will induce brain plasticity.

  • Just brief exposure to drugs of abuse -> insertion of calcium-permeable (钙-可渗透) alpha-amino(氨基的)-3-hydroxy(氢氧根)-5-methyl(甲基)-4-isoxazole(异恶唑) propionic(丙酸) acid (AMPA) receptors && calcium-impermeable N-methyl-D-aspartate(天冬氨酸) receptors at the ventral tegmental area synapses(腹侧被盖区突触)

钙渗透性α-氨基-3-羟基-5-甲基-4-异恶唑丙酸受体和钙不渗透N-甲基D-天冬氨酸(NMDA)受体的插入。

AMPA 受体介导中枢神经系统快速兴奋性突触传递，其在突触后膜的动态表达与长时程增强（Long-term potentiation，LTP）、长时程抑制（Long-term depression,LTD）的诱发和维持有关，参与调节学习、记忆活动。AMPAR在β-淀粉样蛋白作用下的过度胞吞和裂解致其在突触后膜缺失，可致突触损伤和功能障碍，与阿尔茨海默病早期认知障碍密切相关。AMPAR还参与谷氨酸介导的兴奋性损伤，Ca2+通透性AMPAR亚型的过度激活能导致阿尔茨海默病神经元的功能障碍甚至死亡。此外，AMPAR还参与tau蛋白的异常磷酸化及神经原纤维缠结的形成。因而突触后膜AMPA受体数目和功能异常可能是导致阿尔兹海默病发生的重要环节。

NMDA仅在神经系统发育过程中发挥重要的生理作用，如调节神经元的存活，调节神经元的树突、轴突结构发育及参与突触可塑性的形成等，而且对神经元回路的形成亦起着关键的作用。有资料表明NMDA受体是学习和记忆过程中一类至关重要的受体。

  • Prolonged drug exposure -> alters synaptic composition at nucleus accumbens (伏隔核) synapses && (some cases) at the cortical synapses

伏隔核位于基底核与边缘系统交界处，隔区的外下方，前方与嗅前核相连，腹侧为腹侧苍白球和嗅结节，亦称伏核，是基底前脑的一个较大的核团，是一组波纹体中的神经元。伏隔核被认为在大脑的快乐中枢对诸如食物、性、毒品等刺激有反应。

focused on subcortical regions[ventral striatum(腹侧纹状体) amygdala(杏仁核) ventral tegmental(腹侧被盖) etc] && prefrontal cortex(前额叶)

neglected sensory and motor aspects but cocaine/nicotine cue-induced activation of them could predict relapse of craving status(复发渴求) && cocaine can alter expression of motor learning-associated genes in the dorsal striatum(背侧纹状体) [in animals]

unknown: whether cortical plasticity and motor cortex functioning are impaired. [in humans]

-> use: TMS(transcranial经颅 magnetic stimulation) combined with MEP recording (motor evoked potential电位) -> to probe the cortical plasticity.

运动诱发电位是一种无创伤性的检测手段，是刺激运动皮质在对侧靶肌记录到的肌肉运动复合电位；检查运动神经从皮质到肌肉的传递、传导通路的整体同步性和完整性。

Methods and Materials

  • control group: 12, male, 29-60 years old

  • addicted(heroin) group: 12, male, 30-47 years old && heroin for 2-20 years (no use of drugs in the 2 weeks before)

intact sleep to exclude cortical decline due to lack of sleep

Criteria:

  • using heroin purely for at least 3 years 3 years vs 2-20 years
  • intake of drug at least 3 times per week, lasting for at least a month
  • dosage剂量 of more than 0.2g/day
  • no additional drug therapy to quit addiction so still addicted?
  • unable to stop no drug in the 2 weeks before
  • expression of spontaneous craving or cue-induced physiological responses

TMS:

intermittent(间歇性) high-frequency

10 Hz, strength at 90% resting motor threshold, 5 seconds on, 10 seconds off, 10 minutes, 2000 pulses

left primary motor cortex why left

MEP:

recorded from the abductor pollicis brevis muscle(拇短展肌) on the right hand

20 MEPs with 5-second interval evoked by single-pulse TMS stimulation, peak value averaged.

Two points of baseline value separated by 5 minutes were taken and the plasticity protocol was applied if differences were less than 10% between the two. And then MEPs were measured at 5,10,15,30 minutes after.

The baseline should be stable.

Craving Score:

Ask the subject to watch a real video of heroin use for 5 minutes and then to respond to a subjective 0 to 100 craving scale to express his craving for heroin use.

10 minutes before the TMS

Results

TMS stimulation at the motor cortex rapidly potentiated增强 MEPs in the control group but no in the heroin group at all time points

No finding of correlation between the duration of abstincence(戒断时间) and the loss of plasticity.

But the control group exhibited more variability -> may indicate greater plasticity

The plasticity at 10 minutes after the 10-Hz protocol was correlated to the craving score. the others?

Discussions

the motor system may involve in advanced stages of addiction -> addicted subjects show diminished cortical plasticity

potential mechanism:

  • loss of dopaminergic(多巴胺能) signaling[can dampen抑制 the plasticity induction ] at the synaptic site

  • another molecular分子 player: the insertion of calcium-impermeable NMDAR[has been detected on mid-brain dopamine neurons at an early stage of drug exposure] -> alter plasticity induction

  • addiction -> decreased the intrinsic本身的 excitability of neurons. the failure of current induction诱导 in the motor cortex of the addicts and the increased resting motor threshold -> decreased response to TMS protocols

So the study shows an unexpected change in cortical plasticity of heroin addicts.

-> motor cortical plasticity changes and addiction scores?

-> may apply to therapy

Notice

TMS: on the left motor cortex <=> MEP: on the right hand
