Paper || Methamphetamine abuse impairs motor cortical plasticity and function

2020-05-14 12 min read # paper

Previous

Exposure to addictive drugs -> synaptic plasticity in reward-related brain regions (mid-brain, nucleus accumbens伏隔, PFC)

Exposure to chronic慢性 drugs -> not fully investigated

Main exp

methamphetamine self-administration in rats -> characterize synaptic plasticity in motor cortex

Finding

  • a loss of corticostriatal皮质-纹状体 plasticity in rat brain slices

  • impaired motor learning in the rotarod task

    滚轴(rotarod) 实验: 滚轴实验需要动物在滚轴上保 持平衡并连续运动, 是广泛采用的检测运动协调性的实验

Paralleled

human patients with addiction:

  • lack of transcranial magnetic stimulation-induced potentiation (刺激没有诱发增强)
  • depression of motor evoked potentials (运动诱发电位下降)
  • poor performance in rotary pursuit task

Result

chronic methamphetamine use -> affect behavioral performance via drug-evoked synaptic plasticity occluding physiological motor learning

Introduction

Addictive drug -> insertion of calcium-permeable AMPA & calcium-impermeable NMDA receptors in dopamine (DA) neurons of the ventral tegmental area腹侧被盖

Chronic drug exposure -> alter synaptic transmission in medium spiny棘突 neurons of the nucleus accumbens synapses and sometimes also at cortical synapses

棘突是树突上突出的 接受信息传导的

But they focus on subcortical regions and PFC (they receive projections from mid-brain DA neurons).

Sensory and motor areas receive only sparse DA inputs and have not been extensively studied but still will be influenced.

  • Chronic exposure to methamphetamine causes structural deficits and altered DA receptor function in the cortical-striatal projections投影 in humans.

  • Animal studies show a cocaine-altered expression in genes of the dorsal背部 striatum associated with motor learning.

Drug abuse accompanied compulsive behavior and drug seeking that could result from altered motor-striatal plasticity and functioning.

So:

  • characterized synaptic plasticity at the motor cortex to the dorsal striatum in rats self-administrated methamphetamine
  • combined TMS to monitor plasticity of the motor evoked potential in humans to probe a potential plasticity deficit

Brain slice electrophysiology电生理学:

Rats were anesthetized麻醉的 with isoflurane异氟烷 and decaptitaed脱乙酰.

Coronal冠状 sections containing motor cortex, dorsal lateral striatum and dorsal medial striatum were cut using a vibratome振动棒 in ice-dole cutting solutions.

Brain slices were submerged in artificial cerebrospinal脑脊液 fluid (ACSF) at 27°C for 30 min and equilibrated平衡 with 95% O2O_2O2​ and 5% CO2CO_2CO2​.

For the recording of field population spikes, electrodes were filled with ACSF, and a stimulation intensity of 30% maximum response is employed.

For whole-cell patch-clamp膜片钳 recording.

膜片钳技术是用微玻管电极（膜片电极或膜片吸管）接触细胞膜，以千兆欧姆以上的阻抗使之封接，使与电极尖开口处相接的细胞膜的小区域（膜片）与其周围在电学上分隔，在此基础上固定点位，对此膜片上的离子通道的离子电流（pA级）进行监测记录的方法.

For AMPA/NMDA ratio calculation: the AMPA receptor response was recorded at -70 mV to prevent NMDA receptor activation and the NMDA receptor response was estimated at 50 ms after the peak of mixed currents at +40 mV.

All recordings were filtered滤波 and sampled for online and later offline analysis with Multi-clamp 700B ampifier放大器.

Human TMS procedures:

Intermittent间歇性 TMS or continuous theta burst stimulation (cTBS) stimulation were performed.

High-frequency intermittent TMS (10 Hz, strength at 100% resting motor threshold, 5 s on and 10 s off for 10 min, 2000 pulses) or cTBS (50 Hz of 80% MT for pulses train, 200 ms for 40 s, 600 pulses) were applied over the left primary motor cortex.

Human MEP procedures:

Recorded from abductor pollicis brevis muscle on right hand右手拇短展肌

20 consecutive MEPs (5 s interval) at each time point were evoked by single pulse TMS stimulation at M1 region and the peak values were averaged for each time point.

Two points of baseline values were taken (separated by 5 min) and the plasticity protocol will be applied if there were no more than 10% differences between the two baseline values.

After the plasticity protocol, MEPs were measured after 5,10.15 and 30 min respectively.

Rotary旋转 rod杆 task for rat:

Before the formal test, the animals were briefly familiarized with the rotating rod. The rotating speed was set for each animal to reach an initial latency潜伏期 to fall at ~20 s. Then the animals were tested for four times with a 10 min interval between tests.

Rotary pursuit task for human subjects:

To trace a point on the rotating plate with a probe, and the time for the probe探头 to successfully contact the rotating point was measured in 4 trials separated by 10 min each.

Before: less than three trials (to prevent overlearning) to familiarize.

Results

Impaired cortical-striatal plasticity and motor learning after METH SA in rats

Rats were trained to SA METH for 10 days, recording field potentials in slices of the motor cortex (M1) and the dorsal medial striatum evoked by electrically stimulation. Use TBS (theta stimulation) combined with transient顺变的 GABAAGABA_AGABAA​ receptor blockade封锁 to induce诱导 long-term potentiation and 50 Hz stimulation to elicit引起 long-term depression in motor cortex. 100 and 50 Hz stimulation to induce LTD in dorsolateral (DL) striatum and dorsomedial striatum, respectively.

-> These stimulation protocols failed to induce synaptic plasticity in METH SA group in both the motor cortex and DL striatum, but no difference in the dorsomedial striatum.

-> METH effect in the DL, the preferential target of M1 in habit-related drug seeking.

DL striatum plays a role in motor learning -> test the rats for motor skills on a rotarod task -> learning curve曲线 of animals in the METH SA group plateaued保持平坦 after the second block.

Altered synaptic transmission in cortical-striatal synapses

input-output function, AMPA/NMDA ratio, the paired-pulse ratio.

->

  • no change in these parameters in the motor cortex M1

  • in the striatum, neurons of the DL but not of the dorsomedial striatum showed a decreased paired-pulse ratio & increased AMPA/NMDA ratio and steeper input-output function. -> enhanced synaptic transmission via increased presynaptic release and upregulated postsynaptic sensitivity.

western blot analyses of synaptosomal proteins on both M1 and dorsal striatum.

->

  • increased amount of GluN2A/2B/3A (*都是NMDA受体) expression in both the M1 and the dorsal striatum. -> suggesting the insertion of the heterotrimeric异三聚体 GluN3A-containing NMDA receptors.
  • GluA2 protein levels were unaltered -> indicating the presence of GluA2-containing AMPARs

Diminished motor cortical plasticity and impaired motor learning in human addicts

impact on motor cortical plasticity and motor skill learning.

The 10 Hz repetitive TMS (rTMS) stimulation protocol results in potentiation of MEPs in control but not methamphetamine (METH) group (n=11 for control and n=18 for METH); continuous theta burst stimulation (cTBS) protocol results in depression of MEPS in control but not METH group (n=9 for control and n=11 for METH)

Notably, with both stimulation protocols, the control group exhibited more individual variability compared with the METH group.

MEP 运动诱发电位

the craving score in the METH addict, measured by subjective responses to a craving scale, inversely correlated with the MEP plasticity at 10min time point

tested the motor skill learning ability in addicts with a rotary pursuit learning task -> the subjects in the METH group took more time to learn the task compared with the control group & the motor learning performance correlated with the plasticity of the MEP elicited by 10 Hz stimulation protocol

Discussion

  • METH abuse can affect plasticity of the motor cortex in both drug-exposed rats and human addicts.

  • The dorsal system is involved.

  • The poor learning ability could reflect a reduced flexibility that contributes to maintenance of the compulsive behavior and inhibits recovery.

  1. molecular correlate:

the insertion of GluN3A-containing NMDA receptors that were detected on mid-brain DA neuron at an early stage of drug exposure

NMDAR alters the rules for plasticity induction诱导

A failed induction mechanism underlies the impaired bidirectional plasticity.

  1. the loss of dopaminergic signaling:

Human METH addicts exhibit decreased DA transmission in the brain that may manifest显示 as drug-induced parkinsonism帕金森病

Delayed motor learning ability in addicted patients

To research: whether other types of motor learning than the pursuit task tested here are also impaired after drug abuse

  1. decreased intrinsic内在的 excitability of neurons in cortical-striatal circuits after repeated METH exposure.

The reduced response to TMS protocols could be partly explained by the failure to reach the ﬁring threshold in neurons that are less excitable

limitation:

  • with electrical stimulation, the exact synaptic pathways in the motor-striatal circuit cannot be reliably identiﬁed -> optogenetic光遗传学 tool

  • On the human study side, the TMS induced cortical plasticity can be explained by either synaptic plasticity or changes of intrinsic excitability -> examine which of the two potential mechanisms applies -> reﬁne stimulation protocols that could restore normal plasticity

Summary

It demonstrates an unexpected change in motor cortical plasticity after METH abuse.

Clinical studies are needed to explore the relationships between altered motor cortical plasticity and addiction symptoms.
