Hello, I'm Yinuo Liu (also call me Nicole). I am an undergraduate student in psychology based at the Zhejiang University, China (expected graduation: June, 2023).

I'm working as a research assistant both in CAI Lab and Kong Lab. In CAI Lab, my work focuses on the feature binding in working memory. One of my works has been accepted by the 15th Annual Meeting of Chinese Neuroscience Society. In Kong Lab, my work focuses on language brain networks. I have a poster presentation about it at the OHBM 2022 Annual Meeting. Welcome to check the details of them and my other programs in the research page.

I've always be interested in the language. I am now planning to apply for some Ph.D programs, studying computational psycholinguistics/neurolinguistics, as it combines what I have learnt with what I am interested in.

You can email me for anything you are interested in: zjuonellllyn@gmail.com
